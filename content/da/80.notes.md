## Highlight text

Hold a word to begin the highlight process.

## Create note bobbles

Double click anywhere in the document to create a note bobble.

The nearby text is automatically selected and inserted inserted into the bobble.

Edit and add text to the bobble.

## Sharing

The text between the francis «apostroph» is used when you want to share the note. This text have to be identical with the text, if not no sharing.

## Note Menu

Open the overview of the notes with the right bottom corner movable menu.

## Link to notes

In the overview of notes you can click on the note to open the page with the note.

The note app will automatically scroll down to the note.

## Print & export etc

After your study session you can print, email, export and/or save your notes.

## Import

If you don't find your notes next time your are visiting the site you can import the notes and continue where you ended.
